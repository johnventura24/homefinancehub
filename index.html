About HomeFinanceHub

We're an independent editorial team on a mission to make home finance accessible, understandable, and actionable for every American homeowner.

Our Mission

HomeFinanceHub was created to fill a gap: most home finance content online is either written by lenders trying to sell you something, or so full of jargon it's impossible to understand. We built this site to be different.

Our editorial team researches, writes, and fact-checks every guide we publish. We don't take commissions, we don't sell leads, and we don't have financial relationships with the lenders we write about. Our only goal is to help you make a better decision.

Our Editorial Standards

  • ✓ All articles reviewed by licensed finance professionals
  • ✓ No undisclosed affiliate relationships
  • ✓ Sources cited for all rate data and statistics
  • ✓ Articles updated when laws or rates change
  • ✓ Balanced coverage — we present options, not recommendations
  • ✓ Corrections policy: errors fixed within 48 hours
